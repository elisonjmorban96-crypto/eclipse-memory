# SongSplit — Business Overview

## What We Do
SongSplit is a music collaboration and rights management platform that helps artists, producers, and songwriters track contributions, split royalties, and manage ownership percentages transparently.

## Core Problem We Solve
Music collaborations often fail because:
- Verbal agreements about splits are forgotten or disputed
- No clear record of who contributed what
- Royalties get messy when multiple parties are involved
- Lawyers are expensive for independent artists

## Solution
- Digital split agreements created at project inception
- Contribution tracking (lyrics, production, mixing, etc.)
- Automated royalty calculations
- Exportable legal documents

## Target Users
- Independent musicians and producers
- Songwriting camps and collaborative studios
- Small labels managing multiple artists
- Beat makers selling licenses

## Current Status
- Concept validated with 10+ artists
- MVP requirements defined
- Development partner: DevHouseAI

## Key Differentiators
- Built by artists who understand the problem
- Simple enough to use during creative flow
- Legally sound without requiring lawyers
- Integrates with distribution platforms (future)
