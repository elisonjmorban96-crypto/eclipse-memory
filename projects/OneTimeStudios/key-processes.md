# OneTime Studios — Key Processes

## Client Inquiry → Project Delivery

### 1. Inquiry Intake
- Capture via website form, Instagram DM, or referral
- Respond within 24 hours with availability and rough estimate
- Schedule discovery call if project scope is unclear

### 2. Discovery & Quote
- Understand vision, timeline, budget
- Provide detailed quote with deliverables list
- 50% deposit required to book

### 3. Pre-Production
- Creative brief and mood board
- Location scouting (if needed)
- Shot list and schedule
- Contract signed + deposit received

### 4. Production
- Arrive 30 min early to set up
- Follow shot list, leave room for improvisation
- Backup all footage before leaving location

### 5. Post-Production
- First cut within 5 business days
- 2 rounds of revisions included
- Final delivery via Google Drive / Frame.io
- Invoice for remaining 50% upon approval

### 6. Follow-Up
- Request testimonial/case study permission
- Add to portfolio if approved
- Check in 30 days later for additional needs
