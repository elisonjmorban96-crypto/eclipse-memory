# OneTime Studios — Business Overview

## What We Do
OneTime Studios is a content creation and media production company focused on high-quality visual storytelling for brands, artists, and businesses.

## Core Services
- Video production (commercial, music video, documentary)
- Photography (product, portrait, event)
- Content strategy and creative direction
- Post-production and editing

## Target Clients
- Independent musicians and labels
- Small-to-medium businesses needing brand content
- E-commerce companies requiring product photography
- Event organizers

## Key Differentiators
- Fast turnaround without sacrificing quality
- End-to-end service (concept → shoot → edit → delivery)
- Flexible pricing for independent artists

## Current Priorities
- Build portfolio with 5 case studies
- Establish recurring client relationships
- Streamline booking and project management workflow
