# ElisonInc — Key Processes

## Financial Management

### Monthly
- Reconcile all accounts (business + project-specific)
- Review P&L with accountant
- Invoice any outstanding receivables
- Pay contractors/vendors

### Quarterly
- Estimated tax payments
- Review profit margins by project
- Adjust pricing if needed
- Strategic planning session

### Annual
- Tax preparation and filing
- Insurance review
- Contract renewals (tools, services)
- Goal setting for next year

## Legal & Compliance
- Maintain registered agent and filings
- Document all contracts and agreements
- Track IP ownership (especially for SongSplit)
- Ensure contractor agreements are in place

## Project Oversight
### DevHouseAI
- Weekly revenue check
- Client pipeline review
- Team capacity assessment

### OneTime Studios
- Project profitability tracking
- Equipment maintenance schedule
- Portfolio updates

### SongSplit
- Development milestone review
- Beta user feedback synthesis
- Funding/investment discussions

### ElisonWorld
- Release schedule coordination
- Royalty tracking
- Brand partnership review

## Vendor & Tool Management
- Annual review of all SaaS subscriptions
- Consolidate where possible
- Negotiate enterprise pricing if volume warrants

## Documentation
- Keep all SOPs in this KnowledgeBase
- Update when processes change
